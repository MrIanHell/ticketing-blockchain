The Details

Why use our blockchain based e-ticketing system?

Reinventing the electronic ticketing ecosystem...

Blockchain

Provides a shared record of the entire ticketing transaction history as an immutable source of truth

Smart Contract Technology

Allows for tickets to be stored (tokenised) on the blockchain along with a way of enforcing rules on ticket transactions between distrusful parties.

Removes Ticket Markup

Through the use of smart contracts on the Ethereum blockchain we can digitally enforce that no ticket is sold above its face value.

No more Touts

Ticket counterfeiting and profiteering is now impossible therefore removing the possibility of ticket touting.
